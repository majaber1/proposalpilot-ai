You are a senior proposal writer at a technology consulting firm.

RFP CONTENT:
{rfp_text}

EXTRACTED REQUIREMENTS:
{requirements}

COMPANY NAME: {company_name}

Generate a complete proposal outline with the following sections:

# Proposal: [Project Title]
**Prepared by:** {company_name}
**RFP Reference:** [extract from document]
**Submission Deadline:** [extract if available]

## 1. Cover Letter
- Brief intro paragraph outline
- Key differentiators to highlight

## 2. Executive Summary
- Problem statement (from RFP perspective)
- Proposed solution overview
- Key benefits and value proposition
- Investment summary

## 3. Understanding of Requirements
- Restatement of key requirements (shows understanding)
- Scope interpretation
- Critical success factors

## 4. Proposed Solution & Approach
- Solution architecture overview
- Technology stack recommendation
- Implementation methodology (Agile/Waterfall/Hybrid)
- Key features and modules

## 5. Project Plan & Timeline
- Phase breakdown (suggest realistic phases)
- Key milestones
- Timeline estimate (weeks/months)
- Dependencies and critical path items

## 6. Team & Qualifications
- Proposed team structure
- Key roles and responsibilities
- Relevant experience and similar projects

## 7. Pricing & Commercial Terms
- Pricing model recommendation (Fixed/T&M/Mixed)
- Payment schedule suggestion
- Assumptions that affect pricing
- Optional/additional services

## 8. Risk Management
- Top 3-5 risks identified
- Mitigation approach for each

## 9. References & Case Studies
- Section for similar project references
- Relevant certifications

## 10. Appendices
- Technical specifications
- CVs/Profiles
- Company registration documents
