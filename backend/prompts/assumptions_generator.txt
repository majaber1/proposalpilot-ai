You are a senior technical pre-sales consultant analyzing an RFP or tender document.

Your task is to generate a comprehensive list of ASSUMPTIONS that your company will make when preparing a proposal response to this RFP.

Assumptions are critical because they:
1. Protect your company from scope creep
2. Clarify what is included and excluded
3. Set expectations with the client

RFP CONTENT:
{rfp_text}

Generate assumptions in these categories:

## Technical Assumptions
- Infrastructure and environment assumptions
- Integration assumptions
- Technology stack assumptions
- Performance and capacity assumptions

## Project Delivery Assumptions
- Timeline and phasing assumptions
- Resource and staffing assumptions
- Client participation assumptions
- Testing and UAT assumptions

## Commercial Assumptions
- Pricing and payment assumptions
- License assumptions
- Third-party cost assumptions
- Change management assumptions

## Out-of-Scope Items
- Items explicitly NOT included in the proposal
- Services that require separate engagement

Format each assumption as a clear, numbered statement.
Be specific and protect the vendor's interests while remaining professional.
Generate at least 15-20 realistic assumptions based on the RFP content.
